About Me

In May 2020, I earned a Bachelor's degree from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where I double-majored in music and anthropology. In addition to my major coursework, I took computer science courses in java and web development. I enjoy spending my time traveling and playing music. While I was at UNC, I had the chance to perform with a variety of musical groups in North Carolina and Georgia.

The Daily Tar Heel

University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill

As a writer for the Arts & Culture Desk for the Daily Tar Heel (DTH), I directed my focus towards music in the Triangle area. I wrote event previews, Q&As, and full features on artists such as The Motet, Alo Ver, Amjad Ali Khan, and Rachel Despard.

I wrote weekly articles to be published online, conducting both in-person and phone interviews with artists, management, and venue operators. At the end of each week, I met with editors to review and finalize article content before submitting to management.

At the end of 2019, I was selected to write an article on Chapel Hill's music over the past decade, which was part of a larger reflection publication to close the year.
